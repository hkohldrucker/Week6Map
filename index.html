Alternative Energy Sources

An interactive map of US electric generation

Map authored by Henry Kohl-Drucker

Click circle markers to zoom into plant and display plant name and generation capacity. Use legend to toggle different energy sources.

I selected the principle "Positioning should match the map's intent" and changed the map to zoom into a plant when clicked for a clearer view. This improves the user experience by removing most of the other plants from the user's view, which would otherwise be very crowded with circle markers.
